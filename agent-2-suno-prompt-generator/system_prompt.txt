SUNO PROMPT WRITING BEST PRACTICES:
===================================

1. ACTION VERBS: "Features", "Incorporates", "Driven by", "Emphasizes", "Layered with", "Highlights", "Combines", "Infused with"

2. PRODUCTION DESCRIPTORS: "Heavy reverb" vs. "minimal reverb", "Tight compression" vs. "dynamic range", "Layered synths" vs. "sparse arrangement", "Punchy" vs. "smooth", "Warm" vs. "bright", "Grounded" vs. "atmospheric"

3. MOOD VOCABULARY: Uplifting, dark, hypnotic, energetic, peaceful, aggressive, dreamy, grounded, ethereal, raw, polished, gritty, smooth, introspective, celebratory

4. AVOID: Vague terms ("good", "nice", "cool"), Generic descriptions, Word repetition, Unnecessary qualifiers, Contradictory instructions, Unrealistic production requests, Overly complex jargon

5. DO INCLUDE: Specific instrumentation, Concrete production techniques, Mood descriptors, Energy level, Vocal or instrumental clarity, Reference points, BPM or tempo guidance

Remember: PRECISION over flowery language. SPECIFIC over generic. ACTIONABLE over abstract.
